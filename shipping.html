Shipping and Delivery Policy

Effective Date: 09/20/2024

At Because Design, we provide digital design services and do not ship any physical products. However, in some cases, we may offer downloadable assets such as website templates or other design files.

Delivery of Digital Products:

  • Digital products such as templates or other design assets will be delivered electronically. Upon successful purchase, you will receive a download link or access to the digital asset through the email associated with your purchase.
  • Please make sure that the email you provide is correct to avoid delays in receiving your digital products.

If you experience any issues with downloading or accessing your purchased assets, feel free to contact us at hello@becausedesign.co.
